Important Notes

Use and filling notes

  1. Suitable for use in neutral systems.
  2. For acidic or alkaline systems, please use an additional salt bridge.
  3. Requires filling with saturated potassium chloride solution (KCl solution not included).
  4. After filling with saturated KCl solution, some crystallisation may occur. This is normal and does not affect experimental results. A small amount of crystals is fine for normal use.
  5. If bubbles appear inside the electrode after filling with saturated KCl solution, do not worry. Simply give the electrode a firm shake or two to move the bubbles toward the metal rod end, then proceed with normal use.
